02 Ethos

Built with intention.
Designed to endure.

Artifex is the maker — craftsman, engineer, builder. Every release is treated like architecture: drawn on a grid, balanced on a hinge, measured before it is cut. Nothing ships because it is fast; it ships because it holds.

Proelium is the heritage — strategy, patience, quiet competition. The work is independent and made in the open, for the players, patrons and fellow makers who keep these worlds alive.
